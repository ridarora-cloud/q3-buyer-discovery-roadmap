Q3 Buyer Discovery Roadmap

eBay Live · Discovery Team

Experience Principles
1
Earn the Tap Before You Ask for It
I opened eBay for something else. Show me why this stream is worth stopping for before you ask me to tap in.
2
Make the Unknown Feel Known
I don't join what I don't recognize. Give me a seller name, a category, a price point. Then I'll decide.
3
Make Trying Feel Free
I'll tap in if I know I can leave. Protect my place in the app and I'll explore. Make it feel like a trap and I won't come back.
4
Tell Me Why
"Recommended for you" means nothing. "Because you watched sneakers last week" means I'll click. Earn my trust by showing your reasoning.
5
Different Buyers, Different Jobs
I'm not here for the same reason every day. Show me what fits where I am right now, not what works for someone else.
6
One Good Stream Should Lead to Another
When I leave a stream, I'm still in the mood. Don't drop me. Show me what's next.
7
Build for the World from Day One
I should get the same quality of discovery whether I'm in the US or the UK. Don't make my market an afterthought.
8
Meet Me on Every Device
I found Live on my phone. Don't give me a worse experience because I switched to my laptop. The promise should travel with me.
